Rose Michael
writer  |  publisher  |  academic

My first novel, The Asking Game, was a Vogel runner up and received an Aurealis honourable mention. It was published by Transit Lounge in 2007. That same year I co-founded Arcade Publications and left my job at the University of Melbourne. After a stint commissioning at Hardie Grant I’m back lecturing in writing and publishing at RMIT and finishing my second novel, The Art of Navigation (an early extract of which was a runner-up in the Ditmar awards).
